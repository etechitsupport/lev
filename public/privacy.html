Privacy Policy & HIPAA Notice

Our commitment to protecting your family's health information

Lev Kids Care is fully HIPAA compliant. Your family's privacy is our highest priority.

Our Privacy Commitment

At Lev Kids Care, we understand the deeply personal nature of your child's health information. This notice describes how medical information about your child may be used and disclosed, and how you can access this information. Please review it carefully.

Protected Health Information (PHI)

We collect and maintain Protected Health Information as defined by the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). This includes your child's medical records, treatment plans, insurance information, billing records, and any communications related to your child's care.

Your rights under HIPAA include: the right to inspect and obtain a copy of your child's health records, the right to request corrections, the right to an accounting of disclosures, and the right to request restrictions on certain uses of PHI.

How We Use Your Information

Treatment: We use your child's health information to provide, coordinate, and manage their healthcare and related services. This includes consultations with other healthcare providers involved in your child's care.

Payment: We may use and disclose PHI to obtain payment for services provided. This includes billing, claims management, and collection activities. Our payment processing partner, Stripe, is HIPAA-compliant and handles all financial data securely.

Healthcare Operations: We may use PHI for our internal operations, including quality assessment, staff training, licensing, and business planning necessary to run our practice.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect your information:

Encryption: All PHI is encrypted both in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Our website uses HTTPS exclusively with HSTS preloading enabled.

Access Controls: Strict role-based access controls limit PHI access to authorized personnel only. All access is logged and audited regularly.

Payment Security: All payment processing is handled by Stripe, which is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified and maintains a HIPAA-compliant infrastructure. Card details never touch our servers.

Business Associates: All third-party vendors with access to PHI have executed Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) as required by HIPAA.

Online Scheduling & Telehealth

Our online scheduling system collects the minimum necessary information to facilitate your appointment. Patient data submitted through our scheduling forms is encrypted and transmitted securely. Telehealth sessions are conducted over HIPAA-compliant platforms with end-to-end encryption.

Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website uses essential cookies necessary for site functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or sell your browsing data. Any analytics we collect are anonymized and never linked to your health information.

Your Rights

You have the right to request restrictions on how we use or disclose your child's PHI. You may request confidential communications through alternative means. You have the right to receive a paper copy of this notice upon request. You may also file a complaint with us or with the Secretary of the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services if you believe your privacy rights have been violated.

Changes to This Notice

We reserve the right to change this notice and make the new provisions effective for all PHI we maintain. Any material changes will be posted on our website and made available at our office.
